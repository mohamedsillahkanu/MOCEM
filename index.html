SECTION 1: INTRODUCTION

About the Integrated Hospital MOCM/OTSS+ Checklist

ABOUT THIS CHECKLIST

The Integrated Hospital MOCM/OTSS+ Checklist is a supervisory tool developed by the National Malaria Control Programme (NMCP) under the Ministry of Health and Sanitation (MoHS) of Sierra Leone, supporting structured on-the-spot supervision of hospital malaria case management readiness.

This digital tool captures supervisory visit data across 8 hospital malaria readiness domains: facility capacity, staff competency, supply chain, reporting tools, laboratory diagnostic quality, and emergency blood and oxygen readiness.
