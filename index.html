SECTION 1: INTRODUCTION

About the Integrated Hospital MOCM/OTSS+ Checklist

ABOUT THIS CHECKLIST

The Integrated Hospital MOCM/OTSS+ Checklist is a supervisory tool developed by the National Malaria Control Programme (NMCP) under the Ministry of Health and Sanitation (MoHS) of Sierra Leone. It supports structured on-the-spot supervision of hospital malaria case management readiness.

This digital tool captures supervisory visit data across 8 hospital malaria readiness domains including facility capacity, staff competency, supply chain, reporting tools, laboratory diagnostic quality, and emergency blood and oxygen readiness.

FORM B6: EMERGENCY BLOOD & OXYGEN READINESS

Blood transfusion services and oxygen therapy apparatus

13.1 BLOOD TRANSFUSION SERVICES

Target: Hemoglobin (Hb) below 5.0 g/dL in children or below 7.0 g/dL in adults/pregnant women.

13.1a Screened blood units: Safe blood (screened for HIV, Hep B/C, Syphilis) in stock?
13.1b Blood grouping/cross-match reagents (Anti-A, B, D) and Coombs available?
13.1cEmergency donor pool: List of walking donors or local donor registry updated?
13.1dTransfusion sets: Sterile, filtered blood giving sets in stock?
13.1eHb monitoring: Functional HemoCue or photometer for serial Hb testing?
13.1fCold chain: Dedicated blood bank refrigerator with temperature log (2°C to 6°C)?
13.2 OXYGEN THERAPY & APPARATUS

Target: Respiratory distress (acidotic breathing) or SpO2 below 90%.

13.2a Oxygen source: Functional oxygen concentrator or filled cylinders?
13.2bPulse oximeters: Functional handheld or bedside SpO2 monitors?
13.2cFlow meters and humidifiers: Working flow meters and sterile water for humidification?
13.2dDelivery interfaces: Nasal prongs and face masks (pediatric and adult sizes)?
